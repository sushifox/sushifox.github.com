Your Website Should Be One of the Team

Think about how much easier life would be if you could hire one person to do everything for your business. They'd be an excellent receptionist, a canny salesperson, and a brilliant company spokesman. A whole call centre in themselves, they'd be every team member on your helpdesk too.

Your company's website is all of the above. In some cases, it's even your premises.

And that's as it should be. The days of a company website being a novelty are long gone. Your website should be working just as hard for you as any member of your team, if not harder.

The internet is everywhere. People are just as likely to surf the web from their phones as they are from a computer at home. It's more essential than ever for businesses to capitalise on the benefits a great website can provide.

Why Choose Us?

We specialise in creating attractive, affordable web solutions for individuals and enterprises, particularly where a client might be unhappy with their existing website.

We sweat the small stuff. We're as concerned about your website as you are, and we know the devil is in the details.

Because we design and code, we can ensure that our original designs are faithfully translated to the web — so you know exactly what you're getting, right from the start.

Employing sound design principles, we optimise your design for the web.

We recognise that less is often more, and that keeping your site simple and striking helps you convey your message. We understand that the user experience is paramount, and that your customers come in all shapes and sizes.

We know your words need to make a great impression. If you're unsure they do, we can optimise your text content, or even write it for you. Visit the Text Services section to find out more.

We avoid Flash: while it looks nice, we know it loads slowly, crashes often, and hampers usability.

We test on PC, Mac and Linux, in Internet Explorer, Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari and Opera, and on mobile devices like the iPhone, iPad and Android platforms — ensuring your website works perfectly, and looks great, in every major browser, all the time.

If you need a separate version of your website for optimised mobile browsing, we can do that too.

Programs like Dreamweaver can generate unnecessary lines of code when they build websites. Garbled or broken code can make your website load slowly, behave unpredictably, and look wrong.

By contrast, hand-coding your website ensures that absolutely every line of code is necessary, standards-compliant, and in the proper place.

Your site should be a successful marriage of functionality and form, and achieving this takes skill and experience. A great website doesn't often come cheap, and we know you need to budget wisely.

We'll tell you if something doesn't need to be done, or if we can do something differently to save you money without sacrificing quality. Wherever possible, we employ innovative open-source solutions to add cutting-edge functionality to your website at little or no extra cost.

We pride ourselves on our relationship with our customers and our links with other web professionals.

Working with a small studio, you'll be in direct contact with the people designing and coding your site. They'll keep you updated every step of the way.

If there's something we can't do for you, odds are we know someone who can. We'll help you find the fastest website hosting, and can work with professionals in diverse fields to bring your ideas to life.
